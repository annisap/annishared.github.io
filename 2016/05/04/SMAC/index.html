SMAC computing platform transforms IT investments

The impressive growth of social networking services has made available an unprecedented scale of personal data, social relationships and behaviors that when are measured, mined and interpreted are of an enormous value.

Many research articles identify the arrival of Big Data era in the advent of social networking data, IOT and cloud. The first of two mentioned, are generating large volumes of data in a great velocity having different structures and formats. Amazon’s Echo, a voice command device, is the latest futuristic IoT trend that can hear you even while music is playing and can be used from answering questions about news, traffic and the like to controlling your home such as lights and thermostats. If you are an IoT enthusiastic you can try building your own following some simple steps. Although, IoT isn’t producing a data deluge yet, by 2020 they will reach billions as Cisco and IDC estimate.

Image: Cisco

The third one, Cloud Computing,is the powerful technology that make affordable big data, IoT and many other breakthroughs. If you are a cloud developer, check the brand new Azure (CC platform) starter kits that Microsoft announced in it’s Build 2016 event.
To get to the point, the synergy of the above three technologies together with mobile computing make prominent the third computing platform known as SMAC (Social, Mobile, Analytics & big data, Cloud). SMAC change how business develop and promote products, engage with customers, enhance collaboration with partners and other, compete, and succeed. Big Data analytics, web analytics, social media sentiment analysis, predictive analytics and content analytics have become standard investments in enterprises, so as to read customers minds and power real-time marketing. Generally, to discover knowledge by using these technologies wisely would create competitive advantage.

Image: http://www.collaborative.com/
